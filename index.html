02

File Naming

Every file name begins with the track name followed by the BPM and key. Use this exact format:

TrackName BpmKey

If your track is called "Distant Light", is 114 BPM, and is in E minor:
Distant Light 114Bpm Em

More examples:
Suffocation 80Bpm Gm
The Asylum 80Bpm Am
Change Is A Constant 116Bpm Am
  • Track name Title case — every word capitalised (e.g. Distant Light)
  • BPM Number followed immediately by "Bpm" (e.g. 114Bpm)
  • Key Root note + mode abbreviation (e.g. Em, Am, G#m, C)
Do not include an album number, track number, or any other prefix. We assign those internally.

Alt Mix & Stem Naming

Append the version name after a space-dash-space:

TrackName BpmKey - Version

Distant Light 114Bpm Em - No Drums Mix.wav
Distant Light 114Bpm Em - Drums STEM.wav
Capitalisation rule: Every word begins with a capital letter and the remaining letters are lowercase — except STEM, which is always fully capitalised. For example: No Drums Mix, Bass STEM, Drums And Bass Only Mix.
03

Folder Structure

Deliver all files in a single folder. Name the folder using your name, your proposed album name, and today's date, separated by underscores:

Composer Name_Proposed Album Name_YYYY.MM.DD

Example:
John Smith_Epic Cinematic_2026.05.08

Place every file — full mixes, alts, stems, and cut downs — directly inside this one folder, with no sub-folders:

John Smith_Epic Cinematic_2026.05.08/
  Distant Light 114Bpm Em.wav ← Full Mix
  Distant Light 114Bpm Em - No Drums Mix.wav
  Distant Light 114Bpm Em - No Melody Mix.wav
  Distant Light 114Bpm Em - Underscore Mix.wav
  Distant Light 114Bpm Em - Drums And Bass Only Mix.wav
  Distant Light 114Bpm Em - Drums STEM.wav
  Distant Light 114Bpm Em - Bass STEM.wav
  Distant Light 114Bpm Em - [Instrument] STEM.wav
  Suffocation 80Bpm Gm.wav ← next track Full Mix
  Suffocation 80Bpm Gm - No Drums Mix.wav
  ... and so on for all tracks
04

Full Mix & Alts

The following versions are required for every track:

  • Full Mix Required Your complete final mix
  • No Drums Mix Required Remove all drums and percussion
  • No Melody Mix Required Remove lead melodies and main melodic elements
  • Underscore Mix Required Stripped-down version — pads, atmosphere and texture only
  • Drums And Bass Only Mix Required Drums and bass in isolation
  • No Vocals Mix Vocal tracks only Full instrumental, no vocal elements
All alt mixes must sum to the same level as the Full Mix. Do not rebalance or re-process individual elements when removing them.
05

Stems

Deliver grouped stems — not individual instrument tracks. Group related elements together. The word STEM must be fully capitalised in every file name.

  • Drums STEM All drum and percussion layers combined
  • Bass STEM All bass elements combined
  • Keys STEM Piano, keys, organ (if applicable)
  • Synths STEM Synthesiser layers (if applicable)
  • Strings STEM String section (if applicable)
  • Brass STEM Brass and woodwind (if applicable)
  • Guitar STEM Electric and acoustic guitar (if applicable)
  • SFX STEM Sound design, atmospheres, risers, impacts
  • Pads STEM Background pads and drones (if applicable)
  • Vocals STEM Vocal tracks only All vocal elements combined
Only deliver stems relevant to your track — do not include empty or near-silent files. When all stems are summed together, the result must match the Full Mix exactly.
06

Cut Downs

Cut downs are not required for every album. If you are unsure whether your album requires them, please ask before you begin delivery.

When requested, deliver the following durations:

  • 30 second Named TrackName BpmKey - 30s.wav
  • 60 second Named TrackName BpmKey - 60s.wav
The final hit of each cut down should land 1–2 seconds before the end, with a tail that resolves naturally. Do not hard-cut the ending.
